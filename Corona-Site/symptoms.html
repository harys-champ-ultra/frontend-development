The Symptoms of Covid-19

There are some symptoms of covid, which indicates the person's health behavior. There is also an infographic poster for covid, in the bottom of the page.

  • Fever or chills.

  • Cough.

  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.

  • Fatigue.

  • Muscle or body aches.

  • Headache.

  • New loss of taste or smell.

  • Sore throat.

  • Congestion or runny nose.

  • Nausea or vomiting.

  • Diarrhea.
